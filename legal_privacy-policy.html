We use personal information to:

Fortress 5 Consulting may send SMS/text messages to phone numbers you provide. The following terms apply to our messaging program:

  • Message & data rates: Carrier message and data rates may apply. You are responsible for any fees charged by your mobile carrier.
  • How to opt-out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe from marketing messages. For help, reply HELP or contact us using the details below. Opting out of marketing messages will not necessarily unsubscribe you from service or transactional messages required to provide a service you requested.
